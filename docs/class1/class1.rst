Class 1: Introduction to Fraud and F5 WebSafe
=============================================

Welcome to the Introduction to Fraud and F5 WebSafe Agility hands-on lab
session. These labs are intended to show you the dangers of both malware
and phishing, and how F5 WebSafe helps organizations with fraud
detection and protection. This guide is intended to complement lecture
material provided during the Introduction to Fraud session as well as a
reference guide that can be referred to after the class as a basis for
troubleshooting malware, phishing, and WebSafe in your own environment.
